Halasana Pose

Instructions

  • Lie on your back with your arms next to your body and palms pressing into the floor.
  • As you inhale, lift your legs to 90 degrees.
  • As you exhale, roll your pelvis off the floor, moving your legs back toward your head.
  • Slowly lower your legs over your head, toward the floor.
