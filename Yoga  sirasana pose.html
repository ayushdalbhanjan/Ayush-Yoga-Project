Sirasana Pose

Instructions

  • Bend one knee into the chest, then the other knee, keeping most of the weight in your arms.
  • On an inhalation bring your legs up in line with the rest of the body: hips above shoulders and ankles above hips.
  • While in Headstand, lengthen the tailbone up towards the heels.
  • Root your elbows down and knit your ribs in.
